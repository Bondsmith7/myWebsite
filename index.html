About

I was born in Israel to two English parents who left their home to spend a gap year studying Hebrew and the Bible in Jerusalem. 1 year turned to 11 and they had 5 kids as my father earned his PhD at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. We lived in a town just outside Jerusalem in a tight knit fellowship of messianic jews. We returned back to England when I was 7 as my dad looked for work and we were homeschooled for a year in preparation for school in English. After our second year in England, and our first year in public school, my father found a Job in New York and when I was 9 I went into school in Valley Cottage
